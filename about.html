Who are we?

Radford Child Development, Inc. is a non-profit organization determined to develop a quality, certified child care facility in Radford. Based on our needs assessment, we concluded that many members of the Radford community are struggling to find a decent, nationally certified child care for their children.

Benefits of a Radford Child Development Center

  • A Radford resource available to all Radford area employers.
  • Internship opportunities for RU early education and other disciplines.
  • Highest quality NAEY-C childcare in Radford to serve our communities's children and families.

We are 501c3 pending

Although the 501c3 application process can take several months, we anticipate IRS approval for this nonprofit.

What does this mean?

Radford Child Development, Inc. is a non-profit organization incorporated in the state of Virginia. Nonprofit organizations are given 27 months from the date of incorporation to fill out IRS form 1023 to apply for tax-exempt status. We completed and submitted our 1023 form to IRS.

Why does this matter to you?

Once 1023 approved, it is inclusive of all donations taken in from the incorporation date to the date of 501c3 approval. Basically, this means that your donations are tax-deductible.
