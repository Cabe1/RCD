Quality Childcare in Radford

Mission Statement

To develop and/or support excellent, nationally accredited child development program(s) in Radford that embodies the best practices of early childhood care and education.

Results of our Needs Assessment

In order to evaluate the need for a quality child care in Radford, we conducted a needs assessment with members of the Radford community this year. We sought to measure the discrepancy between the current condition and the desired condition. We determined and documented a critical need in quality child care in Radford.

Of 1300 parents surveyed in Radford, 337 parents could not find quality child care, more than 220 parents were likely or very likely to switch to Radford Child Development Center.
