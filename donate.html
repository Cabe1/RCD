How to Donate

Community Foundation of the New River Valley

Radford Child Development, Inc. is pleased to accept donations through a partnership with the Community Foundation of the New River Valley, a public not-for-profit organization benefiting the New River Valley. For more information on the Foundation, visit cfnrv.org

How to contribute to Radford Child Development Inc.

Contributions via check should be made out to the Community Foundation of the New River Valley with “Radford Child Development” in the notation line and sent to P.O. Box 6009, Christiansburg, VA 24068-6009.

One-time or ongoing contributions may be made via the Foundation’s website at cfnrv.org/donate: Under Donation Designation, select “I would like my donation to benefit a fund” Scroll down to the Childcare, Youth Development and Education category and select the Radford Child Development Inc. Fund.

Donors wishing to make non-cash gifts such as appreciated stock, or discuss a planned gift may contact the Foundation at 540-381-8999.

The Community Foundation is classified as 501c3 by the Internal Revenue Service and all contributions are tax deductible as allowed by law. Please consult your tax advisor with questions regarding charitable gifts.
